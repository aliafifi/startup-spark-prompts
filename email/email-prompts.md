## 2. Investor Follow-Up Email

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Beginner

**Prompt:**
\```
Write 3 versions of a follow-up email to an investor I met at [EVENT/CONTEXT].

My startup: [DESCRIPTION]
What we discussed: [BRIEF SUMMARY]
What I want: [Meeting / Investment / Introduction]

Version 1: Follow-up within 24 hours
Version 2: Follow-up after 1 week of no response
Version 3: Final follow-up / breakup email

Keep each under 100 words. Professional but warm.
\```
