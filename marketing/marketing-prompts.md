## 2. Social Media Content Generator

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Beginner

**Prompt:**
\```
Generate 30 days of social media content for my startup.

Business: [YOUR BUSINESS]
Platform: [Instagram / LinkedIn / Twitter / TikTok]
Tone: [Professional / Casual / Witty / Inspirational]
Goal: [Brand awareness / Lead generation / Community building]

For each day provide:
- Post type (carousel, reel, text, story)
- Caption with hashtags
- Call to action
- Best time to post

## 3. SEO Keyword Strategy

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Intermediate

**Prompt:**
\```
Act as an SEO expert. My startup is [DESCRIPTION] targeting [AUDIENCE].

Create a comprehensive keyword strategy:
1. 10 high-volume seed keywords
2. 20 long-tail keywords
3. 10 question-based keywords (for FAQ/blog content)
4. Keyword difficulty assessment for each
5. Content ideas for each keyword cluster
6. Internal linking strategy suggestion

Format as a table with columns: Keyword | Search Intent | Difficulty | Content Type
\```
