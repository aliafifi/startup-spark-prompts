## 1. Financial Projection Builder

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Advanced

**Prompt:**
\```
Act as a startup CFO. Build a 3-year financial projection for my startup.

Business: [DESCRIPTION]
Revenue model: [Subscription / One-time / Freemium / Marketplace]
Current MRR: [AMOUNT or $0 if pre-revenue]
Team size: [NUMBER]
Monthly burn rate: [AMOUNT]

Provide:
1. Revenue projections (Month by month for Year 1, quarterly for Y2-Y3)
2. Cost breakdown (Fixed vs Variable)
3. Break-even analysis
4. Key financial metrics (CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, Gross Margin)
5. Runway calculation
6. Scenario analysis (Conservative / Moderate / Aggressive)

Format as clean tables.
\```

> **Difficulty:** Intermediate

Product: [DESCRIPTION]
