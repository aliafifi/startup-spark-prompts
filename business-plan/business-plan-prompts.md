## 1. Complete Business Plan Generator

> **Model:** Any (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
> **Difficulty:** Beginner

**Prompt:**
\```
Act as a senior business consultant with 20 years of experience helping startups.
Create a comprehensive business plan for my startup idea: [YOUR IDEA].

Include the following sections:
1. Executive Summary
2. Company Description
3. Market Analysis (TAM, SAM, SOM)
4. Organization & Management Structure
5. Product/Service Line
6. Marketing & Sales Strategy
7. Financial Projections (Year 1-3)
8. Funding Requirements

Target market: [YOUR TARGET MARKET]
Budget: [YOUR BUDGET]
Location: [YOUR LOCATION]

Make it investor-ready and professional.
\```

## 2. Lean Canvas Generator

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Beginner

**Prompt:**
\```
Create a Lean Canvas model for my startup:

Idea: [YOUR IDEA]
Target Customer: [YOUR CUSTOMER]

Fill in all 9 blocks:
1. Problem (Top 3 problems)
2. Solution (Top 3 solutions)
3. Unique Value Proposition
4. Unfair Advantage
5. Customer Segments
6. Key Metrics
7. Channels
8. Cost Structure
9. Revenue Streams

Present it in a clean table format.
\```

## 3. SWOT Analysis Generator

> **Model:** Any
> **Difficulty:** Beginner

**Prompt:**
\```
Perform a detailed SWOT analysis for a startup in the [INDUSTRY] industry.

My startup: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
Competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]

Provide:
- 5 Strengths
- 5 Weaknesses
- 5 Opportunities
- 5 Threats

For each point, add a brief explanation and a recommended action item.
\```
